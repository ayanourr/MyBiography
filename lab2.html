Why Smoking is Harmful

Smoking is a dangerous habit that can have serious health consequences. It is a leading cause of preventable diseases, including lung cancer, heart disease, and respiratory problems. Smoking not only harms the person who smokes but also affects those exposed to secondhand smoke.
